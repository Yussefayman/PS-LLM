Al Rajhi Bank Savings Account - Customer Information Guide

Opening a savings account at Al Rajhi Bank requires a valid National ID for Saudi nationals or a valid Iqama for expatriates. Customers must also have an active Absher account, which is the Saudi government digital identity platform. A valid National Address Registration is required, along with a mobile number registered in the customer's name. The account opening process is fully digital and can be completed through the Al Rajhi mobile app without visiting a branch.

There is no minimum balance requirement for Al Rajhi savings accounts. Customers only need to deposit at least SAR 1 into the account within 90 days of opening. If the customer fails to make this initial deposit within 90 days, the account may be subject to closure. This makes Al Rajhi one of the most accessible savings account options in the Kingdom.

The Absher platform plays a critical role in the account opening process. An active Absher account is mandatory because it digitally verifies the customer's identity and national address. Without an active Absher account, the online account opening process cannot be completed. Customers who do not have an Absher account must activate it through the Ministry of Interior portal before applying.

Expatriates holding a valid Iqama are eligible to open an Al Rajhi savings account online. Visitors with a valid Visitor ID are also eligible to apply. Both expatriates and visitors can complete the entire process through the Al Rajhi mobile app. No branch visit is required for either group. The app guides users step by step through the application process.

Upon successfully opening an account, new customers receive a complimentary Mada debit card. This card does not need to be mailed to the customer. Instead, it can be printed instantly at any Al Rajhi Bank self-service kiosk located at branches across the Kingdom. The customer simply visits the nearest kiosk, verifies their identity, and prints the card on the spot.

New customers who open their account through the Al Rajhi mobile app are rewarded with 1,000 Mokafaa loyalty points as a welcome bonus. Mokafaa is Al Rajhi Bank's rewards program. These points can be redeemed for discounts and rewards across a wide network of partner merchants in Saudi Arabia. The points are credited to the customer's account shortly after account activation.

The mobile number provided during account registration must be active and registered in the customer's name. This number is used for OTP verification during the account opening process. It is also used for all future banking communications including transaction alerts and security notifications. Customers should ensure their number is valid before starting the application.

Al Rajhi Bank offers a Family Account service for existing customers. This service allows the primary account holder to open sub-accounts for family members directly through the mobile app. The Family Account service enables easy monitoring and management of family finances. Transfers between the main account and sub-accounts can be made instantly through the app.

Savings accounts that have no transactions for a period of 12 consecutive months are classified as dormant accounts. Once classified as dormant, the account becomes subject to an inactivity fee of SAR 25 per quarter. This fee continues to be charged until the customer reactivates the account by making a transaction. Customers can reactivate a dormant account by logging into the app or visiting a branch.

For security purposes, customers are advised to never share their OTP, PIN, or account credentials with anyone. Al Rajhi Bank will never ask for your password or PIN through phone calls or messages. Any suspicious activity should be reported immediately to the 24/7 fraud hotline. Customers can also block their card instantly through the mobile app if they suspect unauthorized use.
